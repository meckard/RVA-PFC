News

Gen 9!!

A true Pokemon day blessing, Gen 9 has been announced. We got a new trailer, which looks astounding, and we saw the new starters! The Grass Cat Sprigatito, the Fire Croc fuecoco and the Water Duck Quaxley. Coming later this year, and we can't wait!

Astral Radiance Products Announced

Astral Radiance, our next english set, has had it's products announced! The Elite Trainer Box features Darkrai, with a wicked black and red box. This set will include the Japanese sets Battle Region, Time Gazer, and Space Juggler. We are hoping the Character Rare trend continues in these sets!

Legends Arceus Update

Pokemon Legends Arceus got a big update! The Daybreak update adds "Massive Mass Outbreaks" which are mass outbreaks with ton pokemon at once! You can find pokemon that you wouldn't normally be able to find in mass outbreaks, and even outbreaks with all alphas! Bring on all the alpha Spheals!

Pokemon of the Month

The Pokemon of the month is Ampharos! This handsome lighthouse keeper brings joy to all who see him and with the flowing locks of his Mega Evolution, it's not hard to see why. Enjoy him and let his light shine through you!
